GOVERNOR LIMITS

Salesforce is a multi-tenant platform.

Governor limits ensure that one customer's code cannot consume excessive shared resources.

Every Apex transaction is subject to governor limits.

Important Limits

Synchronous SOQL Queries

100

Asynchronous SOQL Queries

200

DML Statements

150

6 MB synchronous

10 seconds synchronous

Callouts

Avoiding Limits

Bulkify code.

Avoid SOQL inside loops.

Avoid DML inside loops.

Reuse collections.

Process records in batches.

Asynchronous processing for heavy work.
