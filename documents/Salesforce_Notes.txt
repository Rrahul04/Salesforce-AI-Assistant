Salesforce is a cloud-based customer relationship management platform.

Apex is a strongly typed, object-oriented programming language used to execute business logic on the Salesforce platform.

Apex triggers execute before or after database events such as insert, update, delete, and undelete.

Salesforce governor limits restrict resource usage in a single transaction to protect the multitenant platform.

A synchronous Apex transaction can execute at most 100 SOQL queries.

An asynchronous Apex transaction can execute at most 200 SOQL queries.
